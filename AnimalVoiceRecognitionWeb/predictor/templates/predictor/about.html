About the Project

Teaching machines to recognize
the sounds of the wild

VoxFauna AI is a research project pairing a Django web application with a deep-learning audio classifier, built to identify animal species from short sound recordings.

Mission

Make bioacoustic species identification accessible to researchers, students, and wildlife enthusiasts.

Training Data

Labeled recordings spanning mammals, birds, and reptiles, curated for balanced species representation.

Continuous Learning

Every prediction logged in history helps flag edge cases for future retraining rounds.

Pipeline

From raw audio to prediction

A straightforward four-stage flow turns an uploaded file into a labeled, confidence-scored result.

01
Upload

The user drags in or selects an audio clip through the upload page.

02
Preprocessing

The clip is resampled, trimmed of silence, and converted into a mel-spectrogram.

03
Model Inference

The trained neural network scores the spectrogram against every known species class.

04
Result & Logging

The top prediction and confidence score are shown to the user and saved to history.

Tech Stack

Built with

Django TensorFlow / Keras Librosa NumPy Bootstrap 5 SQLite / PostgreSQL
