About

Teaching machines to listen to nature.

VoxAnimal.ai is a research-grade AI system that recognizes animals from short audio recordings. We're on a mission to make bioacoustics accessible to everyone.

The Model

A deep convolutional network trained on tens of thousands of labeled audio clips across 120+ species, using mel-spectrogram representations for high accuracy.

The Pipeline

Uploaded audio is resampled, denoised, and converted to a spectrogram. The model outputs a probability distribution across all supported species.

The Mission

Support conservationists, researchers, and hobbyists with accessible tools for identifying wildlife by sound alone.

Privacy

Your uploads are stored securely and used only to deliver predictions and improve model quality with your consent.
